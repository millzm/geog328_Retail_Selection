Customers have a large range of traits. This can include income, height, weight, age, place of origin and so on.
This is an example table that can show you what traits your customers could have. You should have a target
audience in mind, what age are they what are they in to how much money do they have. These specifications
will be extremely useful in helping you determine where you would like to set up shop. If you are looking
for working class individuals it might be better to target poorer areas and have packaging that reflects
the values of your audience. If you are going after children you might want to be near a school or public
shoping center. If your audience is older you might want to select a location closer to that demographic.
